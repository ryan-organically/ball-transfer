Countersunk Flange Mount Ball Transfer Units

CFMC & CFMS

Countersunk flange mount ball transfer units feature a low profile design that allows the unit to sit flush with the mounting surface. The countersunk mounting holes provide a clean, professional appearance while ensuring secure fastening.

Ideal for applications where a flush mount is required or where the flange needs to be recessed. Available in carbon steel, stainless steel, and nylon configurations.
